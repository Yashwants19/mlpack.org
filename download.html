The latest release of mlpack is version 3.0.1, released on May 10th, 2018.

If you are a Linux or BSD user, mlpack is probably available through your distribution's package manager. mlpack is packaged for at least Debian, Ubuntu, Fedora, and Arch Linux.

  • stable source: mlpack-3.0.1.tar.gz (may 10th, 2018)
  • development source: master.zip (from Github)
  • history / changelog
  • build instructions
